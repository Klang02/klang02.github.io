About

Surfside Sweets proudly serves the Central Coast, specializing in gourmet cupcakes and other delicious desserts. Each dessert is handcrafted, and made fresh to order with only the finest ingredients. Surfside Sweets is committed to making your celebration as special as you envisioned it to be and is dedicated to providing you with the quality and service you desire and deserve. Making your life a little sweeter!

Meet Kate DeLange

She bakes sweets that define special moments in our lives. If you asked Kate where her passion began, she would say it all started when she was very young. ‘When I was a little girl the house always had the aromas of freshly baked pies, cakes, and cookies.’ Born from a long line of home bakers, one could almost call it destiny. She went to college full-time and worked several jobs to help pay for her education. After all the studying she discovered baking was a great stress relief! Having a passion for baking and then turning it into a business is a dream come true. “There's no better feeling in the world than sharing something I love with others.”
